Game Rules

  • Each player picks one outcome per game per week.
  • Picks must be submitted before the scheduled game start time.
  • Players get 1 point per correct pick.
  • Perfect weeks (6-0) are tracked and rewarded.
  • Leaderboards are updated weekly based on cumulative correct picks.
  • Tiebreakers determined by most recent perfect week.
  • Standings may include streaks and trends for added fun.
